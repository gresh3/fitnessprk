# AutoAffiliate Pro Brand Style Guide

## Colors
- **Primary:** `#2C3E50` (Midnight Navy) - Trust and authority.
- **Secondary:** `#1ABC9C` (Mountain Teal) - Growth and clarity.
- **Background:** `#F8F9FA` (Soft Gray) - Clean and modern.
- **Success:** `#27AE60` (Emerald) - Good for conversions and positive metrics.
- **Alert/Call to Action:** `#E67E22` (Carrot Orange) - For high-visibility buttons.

## Typography
- **Headings:** Montserrat, sans-serif (Bold/Semi-bold)
- **Body:** Open Sans, sans-serif (Regular/Light)

- [Logo (SVG)](./logo.svg)
- [Favicon (SVG)](./favicon.svg)

## Design Principles
1. **Clarity over Clutter:** Avoid distracting animations or excessive gradients.
2. **Trust First:** Use plenty of whitespace and clean borders to establish professional credibility.
3. **Data-Driven:** Use clear icons and charts to illustrate benefits.
